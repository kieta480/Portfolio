Data & Systems

Executive Leadership Dashboard

Nonprofit Animal Rescue Organization

The Problem

As the organization grew and began setting leadership KPIs, the team needed a way to see the full picture in one place. Decisions about medical care, behavior plans, adoption strategy, and intake capacity were being made by experienced, dedicated people, and consolidating that information and making it visible to everyone at once would help the whole team move faster and plan further ahead.

What I Built

A multi-tab executive dashboard pulling data directly from our shelter management system, accounting software, and payroll system into a single Google Sheets interface with an Apps Script backend. Includes real-time adoption, intake, foster, behavioral, and financial metrics. Rebuilt once from scratch to improve usability after the initial version went live.

The Impact

  • Years of consolidated data now accessible to all leadership
  • Decisions grounded in real data instead of gut feeling
  • Staff can step away without missing critical information
  • Financial visibility expanded beyond one person
  • Built entirely with free tools on my own time

Operational Tools & Systems

Shift Management Dashboard Suite

Animal Rescue Organization

The Problem

The frontline team was doing excellent, high-energy work every shift, and they deserved tools that matched their dedication. Shift information was spread across multiple places, checklists lived on paper, and adoption appointments required manually checking a separate system. A centralized shift tool would let the team focus more energy on the animals and less on logistics.

What I Built

Two separate role-based shift dashboards, one for kennel staff and one for front office staff, each with a PIN-based login, shift type and side selection, live behavior and medical reminders, a team checklist with daily and weekly task tracking, a real-time shift updates feed, and an Acuity scheduling integration that pulls today's adoption appointments automatically. A reporting tab gives supervisors monthly completion data by staff member.

The Impact

  • Eliminated paper checklists across both departments
  • Supervisors can track task completion by staff member
  • Adoption appointments visible in real time without manual checking
  • Shift handoffs improved through structured passdown notes
  • Behavior and medical reminders no longer lost in group chats

Automation & Integration

WhatsApp to Shelter Management System Pipeline

Nonprofit Animal Rescue Organization

The Problem

The team had a natural, effective habit of sharing behavioral and medical observations about dogs through WhatsApp: detailed, caring notes that showed how much staff invested in each animal. The opportunity was to take that information and get it into the shelter management system automatically, so those observations would become part of every dog's permanent record without adding more manual work to an already full day.

What I Built

A Python data pipeline that converts WhatsApp chat exports into structured, import-ready animal records. The system automatically matches observations to the correct animal using shelter codes or name detection, handles errors so nothing gets silently lost, and generates a clean CSV that uploads directly into ASM in bulk. Works on both Mac and PC.

The Impact

  • Eliminated hours of manual transcription per week
  • Every dog now has a complete, searchable record
  • Improved continuity of care between shifts
  • ASM became the true source of truth for dog history
  • Any team member can run it independently

Knowledge Management

Organizational SOP and Knowledge Infrastructure

Nonprofit Animal Rescue Organization

The Problem

A fast-growing organization with deeply committed staff needed infrastructure that could scale alongside its mission. Building a shared knowledge system, with clear processes, accessible documentation, and consistent onboarding, would help new team members get up to speed faster and let experienced staff focus their expertise where it mattered most.

What I Built

A full organizational knowledge system: a master SOP index covering 75+ SOPs across 12 departments, a standardized Google Drive architecture and naming convention, a branded SOP template, an internal staff Hub for easy access, and onboarding systems for every staff role including checklists, compliance documentation, and training frameworks.

The Impact

  • 75+ SOPs across 12 departments
  • Consistent onboarding for every role
  • Reduced repeat questions and dependency on individuals
  • New staff onboarded faster and more confidently
  • Organization scaled from 15 to nearly 30 staff
Google Workspace Notion Document Design Information Architecture Process Documentation
People Operations & HR

HR and People Infrastructure

Nonprofit Animal Rescue Organization

The Problem

The organization was scaling quickly and needed people systems to match its ambitions. A more structured approach to hiring, onboarding, performance, and career development would help the team grow with intention, and make sure the culture that made the org special could scale too.

What I Built

The organization's full people operations layer: a performance review process and evaluation framework, career progression structures with tiered roles and promotion trajectories, full-cycle hiring and onboarding systems, employee relations processes, and a manager coaching framework that empowered department leaders to handle their own hiring and people decisions.

The Impact

  • Launched the organization's first structured performance review process
  • Career pathways created for entry-level departments
  • Department managers empowered to run their own hiring
  • Reduced dependency on a single HR person
  • Stronger culture of accountability and growth
